Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Applied Academy, our courses, and how we work.

Which grades does Applied Academy cater to?

+

Applied Academy provides expert coaching for students from Class 6 to Class 12. Whether your child is in middle school building foundations or in senior school preparing for board exams and competitive entrance tests, we have a tailored programme for them.

What subjects are taught at Applied Academy?

+

We specialise in Mathematics and Science. Science includes Physics, Chemistry, and Biology depending on the student's grade and board curriculum. Our teachers are subject specialists with deep expertise in their respective domains.

How do I book a free demo class?

+

Simply click the "Book Free Demo" button anywhere on this website, fill in your child's details, and our team will contact you within 24 hours to schedule a convenient demo session. The demo is completely free with no obligations whatsoever.

What makes Applied Academy different from other coaching institutes?

+

We focus on conceptual understanding rather than rote memorisation. Our batch sizes are small to ensure individual attention. We provide regular assessments, personalised feedback, and free study material. Our track record of 90% grade improvement speaks for itself. The environment is positive, stress-free, and genuinely enjoyable — students look forward to coming to class.

Do you provide study material and revision notes?

+

Yes! All enrolled students receive comprehensive study material including chapter-wise notes, formula sheets, practice question banks, previous year papers, and revision PDFs — all at no extra cost. This material is prepared exclusively by our teachers.

Are classes conducted online or offline?

+

Applied Academy primarily conducts offline classes at our centre in Noida, which allows for the personal interaction and hands-on teaching that makes our approach so effective. Please contact us directly for any online options available.

How large are the batches?

+

We keep our batches small and focused to ensure every student receives individual attention. This is central to why our students see such significant grade improvements. Contact us for current batch availability and timings.

Do you offer JEE and NEET preparation?

+

Yes! We offer JEE and NEET foundation courses starting from Class 9. Building strong Math and Science fundamentals early is crucial for success in these competitive exams, and our curriculum is designed with this long-term goal in mind.

What are the timings and fee structure?

+

Class timings and fees are discussed during the demo session and can be customised based on your child's grade and requirements. Please contact us at +91 88512 84700 or fill the contact form to get detailed information.
