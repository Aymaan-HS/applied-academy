Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Applied Academy, our courses, and how we work.

Which grades does Applied Academy cater to?

Applied Academy provides expert coaching for students from Class 6 to Class 12. Whether your child is in middle school building foundations or in senior school preparing for boards and competitive exams, we have a tailored programme for them.

What subjects are taught at Applied Academy?

We specialise in Mathematics and Science. Science includes Physics, Chemistry, and Biology depending on the student's grade and board. Our teachers are subject specialists with deep expertise in their domains.

How do I book a free demo class?

Click the "Book Free Demo" button anywhere on this website, fill in your child's details, and our team will contact you within 24 hours to schedule a convenient session. The demo is completely free — no obligations.

What makes Applied Academy different from other institutes?

We focus on conceptual understanding rather than rote memorisation. Small batch sizes ensure individual attention. We provide regular assessments, personalised feedback, and free study material. Our 90% grade improvement track record is the result.

Do you provide study material and revision notes?

Yes. All enrolled students receive chapter-wise notes, formula sheets, practice question banks, previous year papers, and revision PDFs — all at no extra cost, prepared exclusively by our teachers.

Are classes conducted online or offline?

Applied Academy primarily conducts offline classes at our centre in Noida. The in-person interaction is central to our teaching effectiveness. Please contact us for any online options currently available.

How large are the batches?

We keep batches small and focused to ensure every student receives individual attention. This is central to why our students see such significant grade improvements. Contact us for current batch availability and timings.

Do you offer JEE and NEET preparation?

Yes. We offer JEE and NEET foundation courses starting from Class 9. Building strong Math and Science fundamentals early is crucial, and our curriculum is designed with this long-term goal in mind.

What are the timings and fee structure?

Class timings and fees are discussed during the demo session and customised based on grade and requirements. Please contact us at +91 88512 84700 or fill the contact form below for detailed information.
